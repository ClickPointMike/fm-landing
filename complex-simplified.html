About Us

Founded in 1990, Freedom Mortgage Corporation is a privately held, full-service residential mortgage lender. Headquartered in Mount Laurel, New Jersey, Freedom Mortgage is licensed in all 50 states.

As a VA-approved lender, we have been serving the Veterans community for 25 years and are consistently ranked among the top five VA lenders nationally. We offer Veterans and active service members cash-out refinance loans, streamline refinance loans and purchase loans.

We also offer FHA and conventional loans for non-Veteran homeowners and home buyers and are proud of our A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau.
